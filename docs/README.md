# Mo'Creatures Redux&Redone

---

**Read it also in:** [Español](./README.ES.md), [Português Brasileiro](./README.PT-BR.md)

Mo'Creatures Redux&Redone is at first point an attempt to bring DrZhark's Mo'Creatures to life again, but with slighty differences.
This is my first mod at all for Minecraft, but nonetheless an honor to try to continue such a great work.

| ----------------  |

# Introduction

The idea of this mod surged about a few years, on my thinking to explore my abilities on Java coding, and as I'm a big fan of DrZhark's Mo'Creatures (guess it is my favorite mod of all, I play it on all my modded runs), finally I've taken the courage to try.

The priority is keep this even with Minecraft and Forge versions, and optimize mobs generation for processing reasons.
Then, expand the vast possibilities with creatures and their interactions, of course taming and riding are major features.
One thing that differ from original MoC are that Minecraft Vanilla mobs will be changed or merged with MoC R&R mobs.
